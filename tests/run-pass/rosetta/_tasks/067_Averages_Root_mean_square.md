# Averages/Root mean square

**Problem:** Compute the root mean square (quadratic mean) of a sequence of numbers.

**Requirements:**
- Calculate RMS using formula: sqrt((x₁² + x₂² + ... + xₙ²) / n)
- Sum the squares of all numbers
- Divide by count
- Take the square root

**Success Criteria:**
- RMS of 1 through 10 ≈ 6.2048368229954285
