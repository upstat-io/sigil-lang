# Zig-zag matrix

**Problem:** Produce a zig-zag array. A zig-zag array is a square arrangement of the first N2 natural numbers, where the numbers increase sequentially as you zig-zag along the array's anti-diagonals. For a graphical representation, see JPG zigzag (JPG uses such arrays to encode images). For example, given 5, produce this array: 2 4 7 13 15 3 8 12 16 21 9 11 17 20 22 10 18 19 23 24 Related tasks:
