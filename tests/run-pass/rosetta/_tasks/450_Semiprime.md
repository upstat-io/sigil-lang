# Semiprime

**Problem:** Semiprime numbers are natural numbers that are products of exactly two (possibly equal) prime numbers. Semiprimes are also known as: 1679 = 23 73 (This particular number was chosen as the length of the Arecibo message). Write a function determining whether a given number is semiprime.

**Requirements:**
- semi-primes
- biprimes
- bi-primes
- or simply: P2
- The Wikipedia article: semiprime.
- The Wikipedia article: almost prime.
- The OEIS sequence: A001358: semiprimes which has a shorter definition: the product of two primes.

**Success Criteria:**
- 2-almost primes
