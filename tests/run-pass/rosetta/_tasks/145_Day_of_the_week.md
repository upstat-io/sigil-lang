# Day of the week

**Problem:** A company decides that whenever Xmas falls on a Sunday they will give their workers all extra paid holidays so that, together with any public holidays, workers will not have to work the following week (between the 25th of December and the first of January). Task In what years between 2008 and 2121 will the 25th of December be a Sunday?

**Requirements:**
- Using any standard date handling libraries of your programming language

**Success Criteria:**
- Task completed according to Rosetta Code specification
